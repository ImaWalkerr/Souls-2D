Game info
Rules:
The time of each round is limited - 3 min. Then game will ends.
How to win? It's simple - you need to lower the other player's HP bar to 0.
If the time is up and both players are alive, the player with the most HP at the end of the timer will win.
Controls (EN):
Player ❶
Press A and D to move left - right
Press W to jump
Press Space to use base attacks
Press E to use ultimate attacks
Player ❷ (need active Num Lock)
Press ← and → to move left - right
Press ↑ to jump
Press 7 (Home) to use base attacks
Press 9 (PgUp) to use ultimate attacks
